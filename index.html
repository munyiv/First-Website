About AkiraChix

Started in 2010,AkiraChix is a non profitable organisation that equips girls with not only tech skills but also life skills.
The programme is known as codeHive and currently led by Samantha Wamani, while the co-founders are Linda Kamau and Marie Githinji.
The selected girls get the opportunity to join the one year programme and get a full team of proffesional trainers of different courses they will be learning.
